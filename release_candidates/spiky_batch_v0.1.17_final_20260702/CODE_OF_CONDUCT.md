# Contributor Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and scientifically honest. Discuss ideas and evidence rather than people; welcome questions; credit others' work; disclose uncertainty; and do not harass, demean, threaten, or discriminate.

Maintainers may edit or remove abusive content and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer when a private contact channel is available. Until then, do not disclose sensitive personal details in a public issue.
